MISSION

To become a leader in relevant skill training with recognized certifications with a goal to address the employment needs of rural men and women.

Vision


  • To deliver quality education, using the synergy of theory and practice.

  • To inspire trainees and nurture them with qualities and proficiency which caters to industry needs.

  • To constantly update students and faculty with new technologies using state of the art infrastructure.

  • To promote self-employment among students with exposure to newer opportunities.

  • To promote inter personal relations among trainees and trainers to make learning more inclusive and dynamic.

  • To promote over all development of trainees to make better individuals.

History

Hearty Welcome to the JSS Industrial Training Institute, Talavady." This industrial training institute was started in 1982 to cater to the needs of the rural people in education and employment by the philanthropist and visionary his holiness Jagadguru Shivarathri Mahaswamygalavru. JSS ITI is located in Talavady, Erode district, Tamilnadu in a plot of 5 acres.This institute is affiliated to Director General,Employment and Training,New Delhi. The following two years course is being offered.


  1. Electrical
  2. Fitter

The institute has commendable infrastructure, full-fledged staff, well equipped laboratory and voluminous library cater to the trainees of this institute. We also imparting short term courses cater to the need of rural unemployed youths by offering skill training and entrepreneurship culture in collaboration with various sponsoring agencies like District Industries Centre, GOT, and Department of Science and Technology. The institute already trained 1650 candidates in formal and National Trade Certificate issued for successful candidate. There were 212 rural unemployed youths have been trained since from last 15 years in the area of Mechanical, Electrical,Welding,Plumbing,MotorRewinding.  The JSS ITI, Talavady is also registered as Vocational Training Provider (VTP) under Modular Employability Skill by the Ministry of Skill Govt. of India. Under the scheme the institute organized training program in Electrical, Electronics, Information and Communication Technology since from2008to2013.  The institute annual intake capacity for admission is 60 and every year 80% admission is made. We also achieve 70 - 85% results in every year. Full-fledged workshop has been provided for each trade and 950 no of books have been provided in the library. The institute also organized cultural Fest and Sports meet and other social activities like environmental Cleaning, Gardening and Planting of Sapling etc.

Examination

  • The Department of Employment & Training is implementing National Employment Service Scheme for the benefit of unemployed and Vocational Training Scheme for the benefit of youth for getting employment or self-employment in accordance with the standards and norms prescribed by the Directorate General of Employment and Training (DGE&T), Ministry of Labour, Government of India.
  • While the employment service is rendered through the Employment Exchanges and Guidance Bureau, the Vocational Training Project is implemented through a network of Government and recognized private Industrial Training Institutes and industrial establishments.
  • After the completion of the training period trainees have to appear for the All India Trade Test in the month of July every year being the Annual Examination and in January every year being the Supplementary Examination .The minimum percentage of attendance of a trainee in each subject should be 80% and above without fine, and in-between 75% - 80% Rs. 100=00 will be charged per subject per percentage as a fine. The passed trainees will be awarded a National Trade Certificate [NTC] issued by the Government of India. Subsequently Government of India will conduct a State Level Skill competition test at state Level and then National Level skill competition test to induce the spirit of a healthy competition and the best talent is thereby decorated with Meritorious Certificates.

Attendance


The minimum compulsory attendance for trainees in regard to their eligibility for appearing in the final trade test is 80 % of the actual number of working days.
